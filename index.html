About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training institute focused on preparing professionals for careers in cyber security, technology risk and AI governance.

We combine academic collaboration with real-world industry projects to develop the next generation of digital risk experts.

Our Core Model

🎓

Academy

Bootcamps and training programs for students and professionals looking to build careers in cyber and digital risk.

💡

Innovation

Research and development of future risk frameworks, AI governance models and digital resilience tools.

🛡

Advisory

Helping organisations implement frameworks such as NIST, ISO 27001 and NIS2.

Our Community

Students, early career professionals and practitioners from financial services, government and critical infrastructure sectors who want to build expertise in digital and cyber risk.
